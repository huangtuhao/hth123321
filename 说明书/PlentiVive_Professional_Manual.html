Smart Sensor Light Guide

Recommended Size: 1100 x 550 pixels

Operation Modes

1. Always-On Mode (1st Press)

Stays lit constantly. Perfect for reading or diaper changes.

2. Night Sensor Mode (2nd Press - Flash x3)

Smart Saving: Only turns on when it is DARK and you approach it. Won't waste battery in daylight.

3. All-Day Sensor Mode (3rd Press - Flash x2)

Always Ready: Turns on whenever you approach it, regardless of room brightness.

4. Power OFF (4th Press)

Completely shuts down the light system.

Quick Specs

  • • Dimming: Hold button while ON to adjust.
  • • Indicators: Red (Charging) / Off (Full).
  • • Detection: 120° Wide Angle, 3m Range.
  • • First Use: Fully charge before first use!

Easy Setup

Just place the light inside the dedicated mesh pocket on the cover. It will sense your approach through the mesh.
