Quick Operation Guide

01. Unfold & Fold

Unfold:

Open directly. Auto-locks when fully open. No buttons required.

Fold:

Locate hidden Buttons [1] under the fabric. Press both simultaneously to unlock and fold.

Safety System Buttons are intentionally hidden under the fabric to prevent child tampering. Feel for the joint structure through the cover to operate.

02. Height Adjustment

Unlock:

Feel for Slider [2] under the fabric. Slide & HOLD inward.

Adjust:

While holding [2], adjust or remove tubes. Release to lock.

Mechanism Note This is a sliding switch, not a push button. Maintain inward pressure while adjusting height.

Child-Safe Hidden Design

By concealing mechanical parts under the premium washable cover, we minimize the risk of inquisitive toddlers unlocking the rail. Safety is our ultimate design priority.

Smart Sensor Light Guide

Operation Modes

1 Always-On Mode (1st Press)

2 Night Sensor (2nd Press - Flash x3)

3 All-Day Sensor (3rd Press - Flash x2)

Dimming: To adjust brightness, press and HOLD the button while the light is ON.

Technical & Charging

  • DC 5V Input (Use standard 5V chargers).
  • Red Light: Charging / Light Off: Full.
  • Detection Range: 120° Wide Angle, 3m.
  • Do not use while charging.

Light Placement

1. Locate the dedicated mesh pocket on the fabric cover.

2. Simply insert the light unit into the pocket. It is now ready to detect motion and light your way.

Fabric Wash

1. Unzip and slide the mesh cover off the frame.

2. Machine wash cold (Max 60°C / 140°F).

3. Air dry only. Re-install once fully dry.
