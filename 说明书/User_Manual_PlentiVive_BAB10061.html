PlentiVive Trifold Pack and Play Mattress

Model: BAB10001  |  Size: 38" x 26" x 3"  |  Age Grading: 0-36 Months

1. Product Introduction

Thank you for choosing the PlentiVive Trifold Pack and Play Mattress. This dual-sided mattress is scientifically designed to support your baby through different growth stages, featuring a waterproof, breathable Jacquard cover.

  • Firm Side (White): High-density foam provides essential spinal support for infants (0-9 months).
  • Soft Side (Blue): Cooling gel memory foam creates a comfortable sleep and play surface for toddlers (9+ months).
  • Portable: Trifold design with a carry bag, perfect for travel and storage.
⚠️ WARNING

READ ALL INSTRUCTIONS BEFORE USE. KEEP INSTRUCTIONS FOR FUTURE USE. FAILURE TO FOLLOW THESE WARNINGS AND THE INSTRUCTIONS COULD RESULT IN SERIOUS INJURY OR DEATH.

SUFFOCATION HAZARD

Babies have suffocated:

  • On pillows, comforters, and extra padding.
  • In gaps between a wrong-sized mattress, or extra padding, and side walls of product.

ALWAYS:

  • NEVER add soft bedding, padding, or an extra mattress.
  • ONLY USE one mattress at a time.
  • ALWAYS check mattress fit every time you change the sheets by pushing mattress tight to one corner. Look for any gaps between the mattress and the side walls. If a gap is larger than 1 in (2.5 cm), do not use the mattress.
  • ALWAYS place baby on back to sleep to reduce the risks of SIDS and suffocation.
  • DO NOT cover the faces or heads of babies with a blanket or over bundle them. Overheating can increase the risk of SIDS.
  • ONLY USE sheets and mattress pads designed specifically for this mattress size (38" x 26").

2. Instructions for Use

Step 1: Unfolding
Remove the mattress from the carrying bag and unfold it completely. Allow the foam to expand fully if it has been compressed.

Step 2: Identifying the Sides
This mattress is dual-sided. Please select the correct side based on your child's age:

  • Infant Use (0-9 months): Place the WHITE side (Firm High-Density Foam) facing up. Pediatricians recommend a firm surface for newborns.
  • Toddler Use (9+ months): Place the BLUE side (Cooling Gel Memory Foam) facing up for added comfort.

Step 3: Safety Check
Place the mattress flat into the bottom of your playard. Push the mattress to one corner and measure the gap on the opposite side. If the gap exceeds 1 inch (2.5 cm), DO NOT USE this mattress with your playard.

3. Care and Maintenance

  • Outer Cover (Washable): The premium Jacquard cover is removable. Unzip the cover and remove the foam inserts. Machine wash the cover in cold water on a gentle cycle. Tumble dry low or air dry. Do not bleach.
  • Foam Insert (Do Not Wash): The foam core is NOT machine washable. If soiled, spot clean only with a damp cloth and mild soap. Allow to air dry completely before reassembling.
  • Storage: Store in the provided carry bag in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
